Hamburger recipe

Description

Tastiest burger you can imagine.

  • Burger bun
  • Ground meat
  • Salad
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Dressing (mustard/ketchup/mayo)
  1. Slice the bun into two.
  2. Spread mayo on the bottom./li>
  3. Spice ground meat and stir thoroughly.
  4. Shape meat and grill it for abut 15 minutes.
  5. Lay meat onto the bottom bun and start layering vegetables to your taste.
  6. Add dressings and put the bun cap on.
